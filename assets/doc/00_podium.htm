Grand Green Walk

Welcome to Swinburne University’s Renewable Energy and Smart Technology precinct at our Wantirna campus.

Sustainability is a journey where human behaviour and new innovative technologies are combined with our growing knowledge of natural systems to create a world that “meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs”.1

We invite you to enjoy a self guided tour of our campus where you will see and learn more about the renewable energy, smart technology and sustainability features we have onsite.

The tour is best enjoyed during our opening hours of Monday to Friday 9am to 5pm.

1 United Nations General Assembly (1987) Report of the World Commission on Environment and Development: Our Common Future.
