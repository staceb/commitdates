Sustainable Campus Gardens

Being the centre for Swinburneâ€™s horticulture training, our campus grounds are very important. Not only is the campus a showcase for a huge variety of plants but also shows how our gardens can contribute positively to the wider environment.

The campus grounds are maintained in an environmentally sensitive way by:

  • Using rainwater tanks to reduce use of potable water.
  • Avoiding the use of herbicides, pesticides and other chemicals.
  • Recycling our green waste into compost and mulch.
  • Many local native plants create food and habitat for native fauna.
  • Avoiding plants that are environmental weeds.
